$$ \int \frac{1}{x^2+6x+12} dx 
= \int \frac{1}{(x+3)^2+3} dx 
= \begin{vmatrix} u = x+3 \\
                 du = dx \end{vmatrix} 
= \int \frac{1}{u^2+3} du 
= \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \arctg{\frac{x+3}{\sqrt{3}}}
= \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \arctg{\left( \frac{x}{\sqrt{3}} + \sqrt{3} \right)} $$
