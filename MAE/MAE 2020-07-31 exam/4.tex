= e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin^2x}{x-\sin{x}}} 
= e^{\frac{0}{0}}
= e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\frac{2\sin{x}\cos{x}}{1-\cos{x}}} 
= $$

$$ = e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\frac{\sin{2x}}{1-\cos{x}}}
= e^{\frac{0}{0}}
= e^{\lim\limits_{x \to 0}\frac{2\cos{2x}}{\sin{x}}} $$
