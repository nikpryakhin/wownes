$ y_1 = x^2-c^2, \ y_2 = c^2-x^3, \ c>0, \ S_{y_1y_2}=72, \ c=? $

$$ S_{y_1y_2}
= 72
= \int \left( c^2-x^2-x^2+c^2- \right)dx 
= 2\left( \int c^2dx - \int x^2dx \right)
= 2xc^2 - \frac{2x^3}{3} $$

$$ 72 = 2xc^2 - \frac{2x^3}{3} $$
$$ 36 = xc^2 - \frac{x^3}{3} $$
$$ c^2 = \frac{36}{x} - \frac{x^2}{3} = \frac{108-x^3}{3x}, \ x=3 $$
$$ c^2 = 12 - 3 = 9$$
$$ c = 3 $$
