$$ \int_{0}^{1} \ln{x} dx $$

$$ \begin{Vmatrix} \int f \,dg = fg - \int g \ df \end{Vmatrix} =
   \begin{Vmatrix} f = \ln{x} &  g = x \\
                  df = \frac{1}{x}dx  & dg = dx \end{Vmatrix} $$

$$ \int_{0}^{1} \ln{x} dx
= x\ln{x} - \int dx 
= x(\ln{x} - 1) \bigg\vert_{0}^{1}
= -1 $$
